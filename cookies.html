Cookie Policy

A few words about cookies

Cookies are text files that we use to store a small amount of information on your computer. We use them to allow you to log in and out of your account. Without them, these features wouldn't work.

What kind of cookies does Searchness use?

Searchness uses only first-party cookies and for the sole purpose of making the service functional. We don't use any third-party cookies, neither for tracking nor advertising purposes. That's why we don't annoy you with one of those irritating cookie banners!
