Subprocessor Agreement

To perform its Service, Searchness uses a number of third-party entities with whom it may share some of your personal data for processing. Under the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), these third-party entities are called data subprocessors.

This page provides a list of the data subprocessors we use, along with information about their location, the type of personal data we may share with them and the data processing agreements effective between them and us.

Amazon Web Services

aws.amazon.com

  • Country: United States.
  • Purpose: Data center infrastructure.
  • Data disclosed: user accounts and content.
  • Data security certifications: ISO/IEC 27001, SOC 1 TYPE II, SOC 2 TYPE II.
  • Safeguards for data transfers outside the EU: Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Data processing agreement: signed, incorporated into terms.
